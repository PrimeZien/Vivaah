About Us

Who We Are

Originally established as a spin-off of University of Moartuwa, Vivaah Wedding planners represents the synergy between knowledge acquired in academic research activities and years of experience in event management. The company has now evolved to offer both international and island wide consulting services supported by bespoke human and machinery solutions.

Our company was born in the heart of Mount Lavinia in the Dehiwala region, where some of the most wedding planners and designers in the world come from. We have grown in this environment surrounded by beautiful interior and outdoor wedding receptions, rapidly changing designs and turned our passion for event management into our job.

Highly oriented towards new challenges, Vivaah’s mission is to provide innovative, well designed and planned events by recreating the heartheld dreams of our clients and give them a memorable matrimonial memory.

The strong relationship we have with top-tier reception companies is testimonial to the high level of skill and quality of services offered to our clients.

Our Accomplishments

Since 2012, we have been Hilton Group Of Hotels’ alliance partner and in 2013 we won the 1st prize at Asia Pacific Stevie Awards 2013 as the Best Enterprise Event Management Platform of Asia Pacific Continent..

Vivaah has been pursuing a long term event management certification program, by covering all three levels of expertise, from the entry level Associate Designer to the more experienced Designer Architect.
